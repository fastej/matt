matt is an award winning director and theatre maker
originally from north yorkshire, now based in london.

he trained at east 15 acting school and was awarded
the bryan forbes bursary under the mentorship of michael attenborough CBE.

matt is an associate of the national youth theatre
and workshop facilitator for organisations including
the old vic, almeida and tricycle theatres.
