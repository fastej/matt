about matt

growing up in north yorkshire on a diet of roald dahl, chris rea and my granddad's tall tales i always knew i wanted to tell stories. now based in london i trained at east 15 acting school, on the national theatre directors course and was national theatre new works department resident director 2018/19. i'm excited by work that explores class and experiments with form. i aim to create performances that are mischievous, collaborative and full of heart. inclusion and participation is another important strand to my practice. i regularly lead workshops for orginisations including the old vic, almeida and national theatre learning. i've directed productions at a variety of drama schools and am a proud tutor with open door.

acting coaching
collaboration with actors is at the core of my practice - creating a space which feels safe, supported and focused in order to empower clear, bold and playful choices. i offer coaching sessions to help prepare for drama school auditions, self tapes/meetings and any other creative problem-solving you might need.

if you'd like to chat about us working together you can contact me here.

i'm delighted to be represented by rachel chambers at jonathan arun group, who can be found here.

i'm passionate about working with emerging artists and actors in training. i'm excited to develop and direct work in spaces where you can be playful, curious and brave. i've been lucky enough to work with training institutions including east 15, arts ed and the national youth theatre rep company.
